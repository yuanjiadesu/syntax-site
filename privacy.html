privacy policy

Effective date: February 28, 2026

## overview

Syntax is a local-only fitness tracker. Your data stays on your device. We do not operate servers, collect analytics, or run ads.

## data storage

All workout logs, routines, settings, and history are stored locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded to any cloud service by Syntax itself.

## apple healthkit

Syntax can read and write data to Apple Health with your permission:

  • Read: Workout history and heart rate data, so you can view all your training in one place.
  • Write: Strength training workouts you log in Syntax, so they appear in Apple Health alongside your other activities.

HealthKit data is accessed only on-device and is never sent to external servers by Syntax. You can revoke HealthKit access at any time in Settings > Health > Data Access.

## ai features

Syntax includes an optional AI coach powered by Google Gemini. When you use the AI coach:

  • Messages you send (which may include workout context) are routed through our secure proxy to the AI provider.
  • We do not store or log your AI conversations on our servers.
  • Messages are processed by Google Gemini. Refer to Google's AI terms for how they handle data.

## subscriptions & purchases

Syntax Pro subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). We do not collect or store your payment information. Subscription status is verified on-device through the platform's native APIs.

## analytics & tracking

Syntax does not include any analytics SDKs, tracking pixels, advertising frameworks, or crash reporting tools. We collect zero data about your usage.

## third-party services

Syntax does not integrate with any third-party services other than the optional AI providers you configure yourself and Apple HealthKit.

## children's privacy

Syntax does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

## changes to this policy

If we update this policy, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date.
