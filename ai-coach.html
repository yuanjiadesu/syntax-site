how the ai coach works

not a template engine

Most workout apps generate programs from fixed algorithms. Fitbod rotates muscle groups on a schedule. Freeletics picks from a library of pre-built circuits. They feel personalised, but they are not — they are template engines with a settings page.

Syntax's AI coach is different. It is a conversational AI that reads your actual training history — every set, every exercise, every session. It understands your available equipment, your recent volume, and your goals. When it writes a program, it writes one that is genuinely yours.

how it works

  • You tell it what you need in natural language — "give me a leg day for 30 minutes" or "upper body push, I only have dumbbells today"
  • It reads your workout history and knows your equipment
  • It writes a structured program in markdown — warmup sets, working sets, RPE targets, rest guidance
  • You tap to start training immediately

personas

Choose your coaching style. The hype-beast screams encouragement. The stoic gives you sets and shuts up. Your coach, your rules.

your data stays private

AI coaching does not mean cloud storage. When you send a message, it is routed through a secure proxy to the AI provider. Nothing is stored on our servers. Your workout data never leaves your device — the AI sees only what you send in that conversation.
